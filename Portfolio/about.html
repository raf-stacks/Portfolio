The Developer

Crafting digital products with purpose, vision, and precise code.

👋 Hey! My name is Ruth Asiedua Frimpong. I am a full-stack web developer and UI/UX designer based in Accra, Ghana. My entry into digital creation started with a deep curiosity for how things work online, which over the years turned into a dedicated practice of designing and engineering applications that solve practical issues.

I don't just write syntax; I design experiences. I am constantly updating the technologies I master, ensuring that my front-end interactions are smooth and my back-end logic is robust, modular, and optimized. Following clean architecture and industry good practices is the foundation of everything I push to production.

Beyond standard web development, my true passion lies at the intersection of technology, design, and agriculture. I believe digital platforms can modernize foundational industries, which inspired me to develop projects like AgroNova—aimed at fixing real-world supply chains and empowering communities across Africa.
